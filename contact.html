FAQ

What payment options do you support?

We currently support Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. If you'd like to suggest a different payment option, please give us a call or drop us an email and we'll see the possibility of adding it in the future.

How can I track my order?

We've sent you an email with a link to follow your package. If you haven't received the email, please check your spam folder. You can also get in touch with our customer service via pone or email and we'll track it for you.

Can I return my purchase?

You can return your products up to 30 days after receiving your parcel. For further information about how to return your purchase and the amount you'll be refunded for, please get in touch with us via phone or email.
